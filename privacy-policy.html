Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 26, 2025

Welcome to Rivava. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy sets out how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that is essential for providing our financial advisory services. This may include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Financial Information: Income, expenses, financial goals, loan details, and investment information that you voluntarily provide for analysis.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website and tools, collected through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize our financial management and advisory services.
  • To communicate with you, including sending updates, reports, and responding to your inquiries.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To create anonymized, aggregated reports for internal analysis and to showcase community impact, ensuring no individual can be identified.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Your data will not be shared with third parties, except in the following situations:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with a legal obligation or a request from law enforcement.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, and that of our users.

Our fund managers and advisors are bound by strict confidentiality agreements to protect your data.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All sensitive information is secured using Google Firebase's robust security features. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your personal data. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of data collection or specific communications at any time.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact form on our main page.
